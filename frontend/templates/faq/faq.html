RMM (Remote Monitoring and Management) is a technology platform used by managed service providers (MSPs) to remotely monitor and manage their clients' IT infrastructure. RMM provides MSPs with a suite of tools and functionalities that allow them to manage and support their clients' networks, servers, endpoints, and applications from a central location.
